📖 How it works

1. Select ClassChoose branch, semester. System auto-detects today's timetable slot.
2. Connect EnginePython face engine on port 5001 — detects all faces at once.
3. Start CameraPoint camera at classroom. Click Capture Start at class start.
4. Capture EndAt class end click Capture End. Students in BOTH captures = Present.
5. FinalizeAll present/absent written to DB automatically across all subjects.
